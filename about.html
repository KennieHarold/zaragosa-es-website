About Us

  1. The Grandeur of Zaragosa , Bacnotan , La Union

    There was a place in Bacnotan where deers were abundant. They were known for their impressive horns that people started calling the area “sara ti ugsa ” which means horns of the deer” With the passage of time, “sara ti ugsa “ became Zaragosa; a name it carries up to this time.

    Zaragosa is a rural district located at the southern part of the Municipality of Bacnotan, La Union. At the north-western part of the barangay lies the Zaragosa-Bussaoit Water Basin. It was a dam built in 1980 during the administration of the late President Ferdinand E. Marcos

    The most common type of public transportation in the barangay is tricycle and single motor. Farming is the major source of livelihood of the majority of households. Some are engaged in backyard gardening .A few are in micro business. Those who were able to finish college are employed either in the government or in private offices,others are Overseas Filipino Workers

  2. Cultural Groups

    Majority of the residents of Barangay Zaragosa are Ilokanos. Cultural groups are also present like Tagalog, Bisaya, Pangasinense , due to intermarriages . Bicol among others are also found in the barangay. Iloko is the primary language spoken. English and Filipino are the languages used in government offices and in private sectors. English, Filipino and Iloko are the medium of instruction in school.

  3. Natural Resources

    Zaragosa has a land area of 141.57 hectares. it is planted to rice and vegetables. The availability of an irrigation water system makes it possible for farmers to plant rice twice a year. The residents also engage in backyard gardening for home consumption and for an additional income. No one is unemployed in the barangay

  4. Promising Barangay

    Barangay Zaragosa has a 100% literacy rate. The presence of public elementary school contribute much to the high literacy rate. With the support of the LGU, all children of school age from Kindergarten to elementary are in school. Projects of the LGU are done. Example Reading Caravan, Idol Ko si Mayor. The municipality encourages the youth to participate in activities that promote the mind as well as their bodies. Examples are basketball and volleyball competition are held. During summer breaks/vacation the Sangguniang Kabataan of the Barangay join in sports and other recreational activities sponsored by the municipality. Zaragosa has a barangay hall with a basketball court, childrens playground and recreational park. And a small rest hut. People come here to relax and enjoy the scenery. Meetings and barangay fiestas are also conducted here.

  5. SCHOOL

    Zaragosa Elementary School is a complete public elementary school that offers basic education by implementing the K-12 Basic Education Curriculum pursuant to RA 10533. At present, it has a total of 257 learners including kindergarten. It is spearheded by a school principal and eleven (11) teachers which is composed of two ( 2) Master Teacher II, seven (7) Teacher III, one (1) Teacher and one (1) Teacher I who are nationally funded
